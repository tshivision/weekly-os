Block 01
Morning Deep Work
8–10AM is protected. No Slack, no email. This is your peak cognitive window — use it for design solutioning, complex PureFees problems, and design system work. 2 hours × 5 days = 10h/wk of real output.
Block 02
Work Execution
10AM–1PM handles collaborative work: Jira, reviews, async comms, iterating on feedback. Keep a daily 3-task list — only 3 things that matter. Everything else is noise.
Block 03
AI + Content Window
3–5PM is your creative lab. Build agents, write posts, experiment with tools, document learnings. Treat this like a second job. Don't skip it for work overflow. Tuesday/Thursday are dedicated content publish days.
Block 04
Recovery + Social
Workout at 6–7PM daily (5×/wk). This is your hard reset before the CA meeting. Weekends protect social time and recharge. Sunday is planning day — 1 hour to set up the week.
Key rules: Deep work blocks are sacred — no meetings allowed. CA meetings end by 9:30PM; wind down by 10PM, sleep by 11PM. Lunch (1–2PM) and the 2–3PM buffer are for rest, walks, and admin — not overflow work. Saturday morning is a 2h AI/content sprint. Sunday evening is weekly review + plan. Content creation is non-negotiable — it runs parallel to your job, not after it's "done."
